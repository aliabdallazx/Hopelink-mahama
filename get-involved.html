Volunteer Opportunities

Teaching & Education

Help children and youth with learning activities and skills development.

Time Commitment: 2-4 hours per week

Skills Needed: Teaching, patience, creativity

Location: Education Center, Sector B

Community Support

Assist with food distribution, community events, and outreach programs.

Time Commitment: Flexible hours

Skills Needed: Communication, organization

Location: Various locations in camp

Technical Skills

Share your expertise in technology, administration, or specialized skills.

Time Commitment: Project-based

Skills Needed: IT, management, specialized knowledge

Location: Flexible (remote options available)

Benefits of Volunteering

🤝 Make a direct impact in your community
📚 Gain valuable experience and skills
🌍 Connect with like-minded people
🎖️ Receive volunteer certification
